My History

2016

Joined UIA

My first experience designing. I was an IT technician for a teleradiology firm, Utah Imaging Associates, and one of my tasks was to maintain the company website. This was by far my favorite task of the job, and gave me the foundation of web design basics.

Takeaway: An introduction to web design

Graduated high school and achieved high school diploma and A.A.S. degree at the same time.

Moved to Colorado

2017

Self Practice

I took my curiosity and fledging design skills home and kept practicing. In my freetime and I would design and build as much as I could. I learned not only digital design fundamentals, but also HTML, CSS, and just a bit of JavaScript.

Takeaway: My passion for design blossomed

Moved to Utah

2018

Enrolled in UVU

To further my learning about design, I enrolled at Utah Valley University (UVU) and majored in Web Design and Development with and emphasis in Interaction Design.

Takeaway: Started a formal education in design

2019

Joined Apple, Inc.

I applied and was hired on as a technician in an Apple Store with the intention of eventually using my design education to design for Apple. A lofty goal, but one I was committed to.

Takeaway: Opened opportunties to design

2020

Design Bootcamp

While I was learning a lot at UVU, the pacing didn’t suit my learning style very well. And with the commitment to Apple, I dropped out of college. Still determined to pursue my design career, I enrolled in a UX/UI bootcamp through the University of Utah. It was a fast, rigorous and just my style. I completed the bootcamp with full marks and felt ready to put my education to practice in the real world.

Takeaway: Learned fundamental UX/UI design skills

2021

Designing for Apple

My dream came true. While still working in Apple Retail, I applied internally and was hired to design for Apple on a 6 month contract. I worked on the Claris Development team, primarily designing for their newest product, Claris Studio.

Takeaway: Gained real world UX design experience
