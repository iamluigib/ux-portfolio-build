My Design History

2023

Joined Lucid Software as a UX Designer

After months of job applications and dozens of turn downs, I was selected to join Lucid Software as an Associate Web User Experince Designer. I largely attribute my success getting this job to the weekly design pow wows I attend.

Takeaway: My first permanent full time job as a UX designer

2022

Designing for Apple (Claris)

My dream came true. While still working in Apple Retail, I applied internally and was hired to design for Apple on a 6 month experience. I worked on the Claris Development team, primarily designing for their newest product, Claris Studio.

Takeaway: Gained real world UX design experience

2020

Design Bootcamp

While working at Apple, I enrolled in a UX/UI bootcamp through the University of Utah. It was a fast, rigorous and just my style. I completed the bootcamp with full marks and felt ready to start designing experinces in the real world.

Takeaway: Learned fundamental UX/UI design skills

2019

Joined Apple, Inc.

I applied and was hired on as a technician in an Apple Store with the intention of eventually using my design education to design for Apple. A lofty goal, but one I was committed to.

Takeaway: Opened opportunties to design

2018

Enrolled in UVU

To further my learning about design, I enrolled at Utah Valley University (UVU) and majored in Web Design and Development with and emphasis in Interaction Design. Ultimately, I stopped pursuing a degree after some time order to pursue other opportunities of learning and growing.

Takeaway: Learned design fundamentals.

2017

Began freelance design work

I took my curiosity and fledging design skills and sought out practical experience. In my freetime and I would design and build as much as I could. I learned not only digital design fundamentals, but also HTML, CSS, and just a bit of JavaScript.

Takeaway: My passion for design blossomed
